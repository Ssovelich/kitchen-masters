About the Game

Kitchen Master: Cooking Mania is a fast-paced and engaging time-management game where players take on the role of a chef. Prepare delicious meals, serve hungry customers, and upgrade your kitchen to take your cooking skills to the next level. From bustling food trucks to elegant restaurants, master various cuisines and build your culinary empire.

How to Play

  1. Take customer orders and prepare their meals as quickly as possible.
  2. Cook, plate, and serve dishes accurately to earn tips and rewards.
  3. Upgrade your kitchen appliances and unlock new recipes for better efficiency.
  4. Manage time wisely to keep customers happy and avoid losing orders.
  5. Unlock and explore new restaurants with unique challenges and cuisines.
  6. Participate in special events and daily challenges to earn extra rewards.

Game Features

  • Cook a variety of dishes from around the world in diverse settings.

  • Upgrade your kitchen equipment to improve speed and efficiency.

  • Serve customers with unique personalities and preferences.

  • Master time-management skills in increasingly challenging levels.

  • Enjoy colorful graphics and smooth gameplay designed for mobile devices.
